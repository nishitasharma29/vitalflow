Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is VitalFlow?

VitalFlow is an online platform that connects blood donors with blood banks, making it easier to find and donate blood when needed.

2. How does blood donation help?

Blood donation helps save lives. A single donation can provide blood for multiple patients in need of transfusions, such as accident victims, cancer patients, and others requiring medical treatments.

3. Who is eligible to donate blood?

Blood donors should generally be healthy, between the ages of 18-65, and meet other eligibility criteria related to weight, hemoglobin levels, and medical history.

4. How can I find a blood bank through VitalFlow?

You can search for nearby blood banks on the VitalFlow platform by entering your location details. The system will display the closest blood banks available.

5. Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes, donating blood is safe. The process is carried out by trained professionals, and sterile, disposable equipment is used for each donation to prevent any risk of infection.

6. How often can I donate blood?

Generally, you can donate blood every 12 weeks (for whole blood donation). It is important to ensure you have adequate recovery time between donations.

7. Can I donate blood if I have a medical condition?

Some medical conditions may prevent you from donating blood. It's best to consult with a healthcare professional or the blood bank before donating.
