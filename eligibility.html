Blood Donation Eligibility Criteria

To donate blood, you must meet the following requirements:

Criteria Details
Age You must be between 18 and 65 years old.
Weight You must weigh at least 50 kg.
Health You should be in good health at the time of donation. Chronic illnesses or infections may disqualify you.
Medications Some medications may disqualify you from donating. Check with the blood bank if you're on any medications.
Recent Surgeries If youâ€™ve had surgery recently, there may be a waiting period before you can donate.
Pregnancy Pregnant women are not eligible to donate. You may need to wait a few months after childbirth.
Tattoos and Piercings If you've had a tattoo or piercing in the last 6 months, you may not be eligible to donate.
Travel History Recent travel to malaria-prone areas may affect your eligibility.

If you meet these criteria and are in good health, consider donating blood to help save lives!
