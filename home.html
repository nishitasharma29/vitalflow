VitalFlow

is an innovative online platform that acts as an intermediary between customers and blood banks. Designed to bridge the gap, VitalFlow simplifies the process of finding, requesting, and donating blood. It provides real-time access to blood bank inventories and ensures efficient communication between donors, recipients, and blood banks, making the process of saving lives more accessible and reliable.
